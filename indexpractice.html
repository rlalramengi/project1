Chhungkua

Chhûngkaw pawimawhna chu mi tinte awmze neia khawsakhona a ni a. Khawtlâng leh ram nun chhia leh ṭha hring chhuaktu a ni. John Mc Arthur-a chuan, “Chhûngkua hi vawn ṭhat a nih loh chuan khawtlâng thil zawng zawng hi pumpelh rual lohvin a chim dâwn a ni,” a ti a. Hei hian khawtlâng leh ram nun tinuam a, tihrehawm theitu chu chhûngkua a nihzia a tichiang hle.
